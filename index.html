Surreality is a puzzle game set in fantasy worlds. Guided by a sentient tree named Alfrax you will travel through different versions of reality, to the City of 78 Names where you will find the Crow that took someone from you. The Glasses allow you to unlock different perceptions, navigating through each version of reality to solve puzzles. Rifts through realities scatter the world after the Crow landed and the glass shattered, and you are forced to leave the safety of the world you built around you and find out the truth.

But just like the glass, your perceptions are shattered. With each new reality things you thought you knew may change, and places you thought you knew will prove you wrong.
